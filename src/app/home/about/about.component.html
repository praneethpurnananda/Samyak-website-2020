ABOUT SAMYAK

India's Largest Techno Management Fest

KL (Deemed to be University) is organizing the 11th edition of its National Level Techno-Management Fest SAMYAK 20-21. SAMYAK is one of the biggest National Level Techno-Management fests in Andhra Pradesh with a flow of more than 20,000 students from all around the Nation, which has events designed to inspire and inform. Not just these, we have some amazing performances to enthral and entertain on the days of the event, there will be various events and cultural conducts. How ever, we have put a lot of thought and have taken adequate forethought to implement the theme in our college for SAMYAK.

At K L Deemed to be University
